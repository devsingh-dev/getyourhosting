What is Shared Hosting?

Shared hosting is a service where multiple websites reside on a single web server connected to the internet. Because the server resources (like RAM and CPU) are shared among all users, it is the most cost-effective and popular hosting option for new websites.

💼 Who is it for?

Perfect for beginners, personal bloggers, portfolio sites, and small local business websites that receive low to moderate monthly traffic.

⚙️ Essential Features to Look For

Always look for a Free Domain Name for the first year, Free SSL Certificates, Unmetered Bandwidth, and an easy 1-Click WordPress Installer.
