Web Hosting Services

The Foundation of Your Online Presence

What is Web Hosting?

Web hosting is an online service that allows you to publish your website files onto the internet. When you purchase a hosting plan, you are basically renting space on a physical server that runs 24/7 so that your website is available to users worldwide at any time.

How Does It Work?

1. Storage

Your website's code, images, and databases are stored on a high-powered computer called a server.

2. DNS Link

When a user types your URL (Domain Name), their browser connects to the server's IP address.

3. Delivery

The server "serves" the requested files to the user's screen, displaying your website.
