Bluehost Review (2026)

The Official WordPress Recommendation

>

If you have ever researched how to start a blog, you have almost certainly come across Bluehost. They are one of the top choices for WordPress users, largely because WordPress officially recommends them. But is their massive popularity justified, or is it just good marketing? Let's dive into the details.

Standout Features

1. Seamless WordPress Setup

Bluehost makes launching a WordPress site foolproof. Their onboarding process guides you step-by-step, automatically installs WordPress, and helps you pick a theme right from the dashboard.

2. Free Domain Name

To help beginners get started quickly, Bluehost includes a free domain name for your first year on all of their hosting plans. This saves you the hassle of connecting a domain from a third-party registrar.

3. Built-in Analytics & SEO

The dashboard provides built-in tools to monitor your website traffic and basic SEO recommendations, giving beginners a head start on getting their site to rank on Google.

Pricing Breakdown

Hosting Plan Starting Price Best For Key Features
Starter ₹371.59/mo* Perfect for your first website or blog. 10 Websites, 10 GB NVMe SSD, AI Site Creation, Free Domain (1st yr).
Business ₹650.98/mo* The perfect pairing of power and resources. 50 Websites, 50 GB NVMe SSD, Phone Support, Domain Privacy (1st yr).
eCommerce Essentials ₹1,396.02/mo* Essential commerce tools to sell your content online. 100 Websites, 100 GB NVMe SSD, Secure Payments, WooCommerce Auto-Install.

*Prices shown are for a 36-month term.

Pros & Cons

The Good

  • Generous allowance of 10 websites even on the base plan.
  • Fast NVMe SSD storage across all tiers.
  • Free AI Site Creation Tools and Free Site Migration included.
  • Advanced WordPress tools like Object Caching and Staging Sites.
  • Free Domain for the 1st year on all plans.

The Catch

  • Steep renewal prices (e.g., Starter renews at ₹930.37/mo).
  • Best advertised pricing requires a long 36-month commitment.
  • Phone support is excluded from the Starter plan (Chat only).
  • Pro Email is only included as a Free Trial.

Final Verdict

Bluehost provides a highly optimized environment for WordPress users, complete with AI site creation tools, automatic updates, and built-in caching. It is exceptionally generous with website limits—offering up to 10 sites on its base tier. While the initial features and 99.99% Uptime SLA are fantastic, you must be prepared to commit to 36 months to get the lowest rate and budget for higher prices upon renewal.
