MilesWeb Review (2026)

High-Performance Hosting with Local Indian Support

MilesWeb has emerged as a powerhouse in the web hosting industry, especially known for its localized servers and aggressive pricing. Whether you're looking for shared hosting or powerful VPS solutions, MilesWeb promises "Your Hosting, Our Passion." But does their performance live up to the hype for 2026?

Standout Features

1. LiteSpeed Web Server

MilesWeb uses LiteSpeed servers which are significantly faster than traditional Apache servers, ensuring your WordPress site loads in a blink.

2. 24/7/365 Local Support

One of their biggest strengths is their support team. You get instant assistance via live chat and email in multiple languages, including Hindi and English.

3. Free SSL & Backups

Security isn't an afterthought. Every plan comes with a free Let's Encrypt SSL certificate and automated daily backups to keep your data secure.

Pros & Cons

The Good

  • Guaranteed Renewal Price: You pay the same price when you renew.
  • Generous NVMe storage across all tiers.
  • Free AI website builder, AI credits, and AI WP speed boost included.
  • LiteSpeed Server + CDN on every plan for fast loading.
  • Free website migration, daily backups, and 24/7 priority support.

The Catch

  • The entry-level "Starter" plan does not include a free domain name.
  • Node.js support is only available on the higher-tier Max and Cloud Pro plans.

Final Verdict

MilesWeb offers highly competitive pricing, especially with their "Same Price at Renewal" guarantee, which is rare in the hosting industry. Packed with modern features like NVMe storage, LiteSpeed servers, and built-in AI tools, these plans offer excellent value. It is a top-tier choice if you are targeting an audience in India, thanks to their local server locations and robust priority support.
