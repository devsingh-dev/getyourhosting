What is Reseller Hosting?

Reseller hosting allows you to purchase hosting resources (disk space and bandwidth) in bulk from a parent provider, and then divide and sell them to your own clients. You act as the hosting company, with your own brand name and pricing, while the parent company manages the physical server hardware.

💼 Who is it for?

Web design agencies, developers, and freelancers who manage multiple client websites and want to earn recurring monthly revenue by offering them hosting services.

⚙️ Essential Features to Look For

Always look for 100% White-Labeling (so your clients don't see the parent company), client billing software compatibility, and an easy control panel to manage sub-accounts.
